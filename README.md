📦 Supply Chain Performance Analysis – AtliQ Mart
📌 Project Overview

AtliQ Mart, an FMCG manufacturer operating in Gujarat, is facing customer churn due to poor service levels. Key customers are not renewing contracts due to late deliveries and incomplete orders.

This project focuses on analyzing On-Time (OT%), In-Full (IF%), and OTIF% metrics to identify service gaps, uncover root causes, and provide data-driven recommendations before expansion to Tier-1 cities.

🎯 Objective
Track service level performance across customers, cities, and time
Identify root causes behind service failures
Highlight high-risk customers likely to churn
Enable stakeholders to take proactive corrective actions

🔍 Key Insights
OTIF% is significantly below target → core reason for customer dissatisfaction
Late deliveries (OT failures) are the primary driver of service issues
In-Full (IF%) is also below target → indicates inventory or fulfillment gaps
Certain customers consistently receive poor service → high churn risk
Delay buckets show majority failures in 1–2 day late deliveries
Some product categories contribute disproportionately to failures
City-level variation exists → operational inconsistency
